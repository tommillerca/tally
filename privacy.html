Boneheadz Gym — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Boneheadz Gym is a personal calorie and habit tracker that turns healthy routines into a game. This app is an independent personal project. It is not affiliated with, operated by, or connected to any company. Your privacy is simple here: your data is yours, and anything backed up off your phone is end-to-end encrypted so that only your device can read it.

What the app stores

  • On your device. Your food log, weigh-ins, targets, steps, and all game progress (XP, gear, pets, coins, quests) are stored locally on your phone.
  • Encrypted cloud backup (on by default). So you never lose your progress if you reinstall the app or get a new phone, a copy of your save is backed up to the developer's server. It is encrypted on your phone first, with a key that never leaves your device, so the server stores only unreadable data. We cannot see your food, weight, or health information. You can turn cloud backup off in Settings.
  • Apple Health / Health Connect (optional). If you choose to connect Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android), the app reads your steps, active calories, and optionally weight, on your device, to power the game and your trends. The app only reads this data (it never writes health records), uses it on-device, and never transmits it off your phone except inside your own end-to-end-encrypted backup if you have backup turned on. You can revoke this access any time in your system Health settings.
  • Camera (optional). If you scan a nutrition label or barcode, the photo is processed on your device to read the numbers. Photos are not stored or uploaded.

Social features

The app creates an anonymous device key (no name, email, or account). For social features like friend codes, and later trading and PvP, it syncs a small game-progress snapshot (level, stats, outfit, gear, badges). That snapshot never includes your food entries, weight, or health data. This is separate from the encrypted backup above, which does include your full save but is unreadable to anyone but you.

Usage analytics

To understand how many people play and which features get used, the app sends usage events (for example "opened the app", "won a fight", or how long a screen was viewed) to the developer's own server. These carry a random device id and never include your food, weight, or health data. There are no third-party analytics services or advertising trackers involved.

If you go online and choose a Crew name, that display name is attached to these events so the developer can tell testers apart during development. The developer's server also records a coarse location (country, region, and city) derived from your internet connection's IP address when events are sent. This is approximate, is never precise GPS, and no location permission is used. You can stay fully anonymous by not going online.

Map feedback you send

On the map, you can press and hold a spot to nominate it as a boss den, or flag a coin or boss you can't reach (for example, on private property). If you do, the app sends the map coordinates you tapped and the note you typed to the developer's own server so the map can be improved. This is optional, only happens when you choose to submit, and is never shared with other players. Do not include personal information in the note.

Survey and email (optional)

The app includes a one-time survey for early players. If you choose to fill it out, you can share your name, feedback, and what would make you play more, and optionally your email address. Your email is used only to reply to you or, if you tick the opt-in box, to send occasional updates about the app. It is stored on the developer's own server, is never shared with other players, is never sold or given to third parties, and is never used for advertising. The survey is entirely optional, and you can ask to have your email removed at any time by contacting the support email below. Filling out the survey grants an in-game cosmetic pet.

What we do NOT do

  • No ads.
  • No third-party analytics or trackers.
  • No selling or sharing of your data (including your email).
  • No account or sign-up required to use the app; email is only ever collected if you choose to submit the optional survey.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device. You can also export a backup of your log at any time from Settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer at the support email shown on the app's App Store listing.
