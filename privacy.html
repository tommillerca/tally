Boneheadz Gym — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Boneheadz Gym is a personal calorie and habit tracker that turns healthy routines into a game. This app is an independent personal project. It is not affiliated with, operated by, or connected to any company. Your privacy is simple here: your data stays on your device.

What the app stores

  • On your device only. Your food log, weigh-ins, targets, steps, and all game progress (XP, gear, pets, coins, quests) are stored locally on your phone. They are not uploaded to us and we cannot see them.
  • Apple Health (optional). If you choose to connect Apple Health, the app reads step and activity data on your device to power the game. This data is used on-device and is never transmitted off your phone.
  • Camera (optional). If you scan a nutrition label or barcode, the photo is processed on your device to read the numbers. Photos are not stored or uploaded.

Optional online features

If you turn on the optional social features ("Go Online"), the app creates an anonymous device key (no name, email, or account) and syncs a small game-progress snapshot so features like friends and trading can work. This never includes your food entries, weight, or health data. You can use the entire app without ever going online.

Anonymous usage analytics

To understand how many people play and which features get used, the app may send anonymous usage events (for example "opened the app" or "won a fight") to the developer's own server. These are tied to a random device id, not to your name, email, or Apple ID, and never include your food, weight, or health data. There are no third-party analytics services or advertising trackers involved.

What we do NOT do

  • No ads.
  • No third-party analytics or trackers.
  • No selling or sharing of your data.
  • No account, email, or sign-up required to use the app.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device. You can also export a backup of your log at any time from Settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer at the support email shown on the app's App Store listing.
