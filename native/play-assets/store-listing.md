## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Boneheadz Gym is a calorie and macro tracker wearing a full RPG. Log your meals, walk in the real world, and a very cool skeleton levels up on your behalf.

Most food trackers are a chore. Boneheadz turns the daily habit into a game: every meal you log and every step you walk earns XP, loot, and coins for your Bonehead. Stick with it and your skeleton grows from a Rookie Logger into a gear-clad Pit champion.

THE BASICS (a real tracker underneath)
• Fast logging: scan a barcode, photograph a Nutrition Facts label, or search millions of foods (Open Food Facts + USDA).
• Calories, protein, carbs and fat against your daily target, with a clean ring and trends.
• Weight tracking with a smoothed trend line, plus water, sleep and step charts.
• Recents, favorites, and copy-yesterday so logging takes seconds.

THE GAME (why you'll actually come back)
• Level up a skeleton from the food you log and the steps you walk.
• The Pit: turn-based combat where you and your pet take on skeletons and world bosses.
• Pets: hatch, level, breed, and fight alongside cosmic hounds, wardens and more.
• Gear and weapons: kit out your Bonehead from the Bone Merchant, salvage duplicates for Bone Dust.
• The Kitchen: cook ingredients you find into battle buffs, and stockpile them in your Pantry.
• Quests: daily, weekly and monthly goals that reward coins, crates, ingredients and rare loot.
• The Crew: add friends by code, battle their Bonehead, and send gifts.

BUILT AROUND HEALTHY HABITS
Boneheadz never rewards eating less. Every reward comes from logging consistently, hitting your protein, and moving your body. Walking is the engine of the whole game.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your food, weight and health data live on your device. Optional cloud backup is end-to-end encrypted, readable only on your phone. No ads. No selling your data.

Feed the bones.
